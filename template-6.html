group & events
Organizing events or group experiences can be daunting, especially in such a sought- after destination as Victoria Fall. We at Pure Africa have got you covered.

Whether your clients are looking to celebrate private events, corporate events or weddings in Victoria Falls. Pure Africa is your single point of contact to guarantee a seamless experience and overall destination solution.

Weddings

Victoria Falls is one of the dream wedding destinations in Africa. With over 25 years of experience in making wedding dreams come true, Pure Africa is the go-to organizer of all-in-one destination weddings. We will ensure a seamless handling of everything the wedding party will require in the most stunning locations in and around Victoria Falls. Types of Events we offer: Elopements, Weddings, Vowel Renewals, Hen & Stag Parties.

Private Events

What better way to celebrate a special occasion or a beautiful event amongst family and friends than in one of Africa’s most beautiful locations? Victoria Falls offers a myriad of unmatched locations, whether it’s on the banks of the Zambezi, in the untouched wilderness of the Zambezi National Park or on one of our luxurious cruise vessels. Pure Africa – as your premium destination event manager with coherent and fully-packaged solutions – will make sure your guests have a truly unforgettable and seamless event from start to finish.

Venues

Looking for the perfect location in Victoria Falls for your clients? Look no further, Pure Africa owns and manages some of the most beautiful sites and guarantees you a “different factor” in style. All our locations like the Pure Africa Waterfront or our vessels on the Zambezi River boasts great design & décor and come with freshly prepared delicacies and top-notch service. We also offer other venues and provide the same standard of service in such.

Corporate Events

Whether it’s a product launch, incentive trips or conference-related events and experiences, we ensure a carefree and seamless experience for our clients. We serve as your all-in-one destination event manager and cover every single link of the chain from meet and greets at the airport, transfers, accommodations over bespoke experiences and events to venues and cuisine. In all of this we incorporate our brand philosophy of design, innovation, culinary art and impact. A corporate event organized by us goes beyond just “corporate”, your clients will be able to enjoy new age event design, aesthetically pleasing settings and delicious delights while contributing to leaving a legacy in Africa.

A favourite amongst our guests, the Historic Bridge & Tram Lunch Experience has it all: history, culinary delights, wildlife and stunning vistas.
BOOK NOW
Description

Guests embark at the beautiful Victoria Falls Station, where Pimm’s, bubbly, snacks and freshly prepared artisanal sandwiches await, before they get to enjoy a scenic ride in a restored historic tram across the famous Victoria Bridge to Zambia. Here guests disembark and listen to a brief talk about the construction of the bridge by a historian, before being ushered to a craft gin pop-up bar at secret lookout point and eventually making their way back to the Zimbabwean side.
