OUR Pot & experiences
Pure Africa proudly presents its very own Pot &, a completely new experience, which has taken Victoria Falls by storm.
Description

On the banks of the magical Zambezi River and under the guidance of one of our professional chefs, participants mix and match fresh ingredients in a traditional Southern African potjie and then embark on a 3-4 hour experience of their choice, while their deliciousness simmers. Upon return, they can indulge in their very own delectable dish in great company and in contemporary environment. The options are Pot & Cruise (plus a river cruise on the Zambezi), Pot & Spot (plus a game drive in the Zambezi National Park) or Pot & Paint (plus a painting class on the Zambezi river bank).

Pot & Spot

Price $

The Pot & Spot is not just a culinary journey, it’s a culinary safari. Your guests can look forward to a beautiful melange of Southern African cuisine and exciting wildlife sightings. To start off: Under the guidance of one of our top chefs, favourite ingredients are thrown into a traditional potjie (pot) on the banks of the Zambezi. While their deliciousness simmers for a few hours, guests will go on a game drive in the Zambezi National Park (spot), where they can observe elephants, zebras, giraffes, buffalos or even lions. Pot & Cruise t

Price $

The Pot & Cruise will spoil every gourmand with a wide variety of Southern African cuisine. Start off with preparing a stew with all your favourite delicacies in a so-called potjie (pot), which will simmer for a few hours while you enjoy the beauty of the Zambezi River. Glide along this wildlife paradise in style on the Luxury or Signature Deck of the luxurious Zambezi Explorer (cruise). After being stunned by the famous Victoria Falls sunset, you will disembark back at the Pure Africa Waterfront and enjoy your very own potjie.

Pot & Paint

Price $

A beautiful combination of culinary art and creativity. The Pot & Paint lets guests experience traditional Southern African food when they choose their own ingredients to prepare a potjie stew (pot), before moving onto the stylish Zambezi House restaurant to paint the stunning landscape along the Zambezi River (paint). Both the food experience and the painting class are accompanied by experts in their field and make this a truly unique activity in Victoria Falls with a mix of leisure and education.
